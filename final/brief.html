Final Project Brief

Project Summary

I will update the Game On! project, because it has been the most fun to work on.

Project Description

For my project, I plan to majorly improve the visual design, as it is the part that can help my game build its unique personality.

To do this, I plan on adding more custom illustrations and animated reactions when buttons are pushed. For example, if a user decides to roll the die again, instead of immediately popping up images, I want to add a transition that makes the process smoother. Without them, everything feels too abrupt, and there is no flow.

I've already included sound into my original design, but I would like to expand them to help immerse the user even more. I plan to make the audio on my own, then layer multiple files to create depth. It will make things more interesting but won't distract from the game.

Expert Feedback

To get further feedback, I will test my game with two friends who have extensive experience in visual and UX design.

Since they are my close friends and have no association with the class, I am sure they will be brutally honest with me. There's no better way to recieve feedback than by friends who find pleasure in making fun of you.
